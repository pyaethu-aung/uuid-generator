## Anti-Patterns Verdict

LLM: terminal-luxury execution passes the first-order slop test. Warm-grey neutral ramp, mono-as-chrome, status bar density — all considered choices. Second-order risk: "dark + mono + lime" for dev tools is filling up in 2026; saved by warm hue 60 tint and workbench density. No absolute bans triggered.

Detector: one finding — overused-font on Geist Mono (false positive in context — slashed-zero is a technical requirement for UUID legibility). No other flags across 14 files.

Browser: screenshots captured for both tabs in dark and light themes. No overlay injection attempted.

## Overall Impression

Excellent foundation. Generator is polished and immediately useful. Validator is a strong addition with a compelling segment breakdown. Biggest opportunity: validator hero takes 40% of viewport for a surface users deliberately navigated to — cut it, and the validator becomes as crisp as the generator.

## What's Working

1. Status bar as persistent, information-dense chrome — "live · ver v4 · batch 001 · visible 01 · flags [none] · ready" gives a complete tool state picture. Validator variant is the best use of a status bar in a browser tool.
2. Segment breakdown with accent-highlighted version field — lime on 7518 against muted surrounding segments. One-accent-at-a-time applied correctly.
3. Keyboard-first design with visible affordances — inline hints in rail heads, shortcut modal as reference not entry point.

## Priority Issues

[P1] Validator hero consumes 40% of viewport for a secondary surface. Users already oriented when they click the Validator tab. Replace with a compact descriptor at --fs-sm or nothing; workbench teaches by showing.

[P2] Light-mode lime accent fails large-text contrast. oklch(0.62 0.22 130) on oklch(0.985 0.003 60) ≈ 2.7:1, below 3:1 WCAG AA large-text minimum. Darken to ~oklch(0.52 0.22 130).

[P2] Invalid UUID error is non-actionable. "Input does not match RFC 4122" names the miss but not the cause. Extend uuidDecoder.js to return a reason field; surface it in ValidationBanner.

[P2] "match" section label is opaque. Rename to "accept" — terminal-toned, directly communicates purpose.

[P3] Hero subtitle paragraphs unconstrained on wide viewports. Add max-width: 65ch to .hero-sub and .validator-hero__sub.

## Persona Red Flags

Alex (Power User): regenerate shortcut not surfaced on UUID panel itself — modal only. No way to lock individual UUIDs while regenerating batch.

Sam (Accessibility): --ink-4 at 10–11px in light mode ≈ 3–3.5:1, fails 4.5:1 for small text. Light-mode lime accent fails large-text threshold.

Jordan (First-Timer): format options use function-syntax labels with no live preview. Validator tab purpose not hinted at from Generator view.
