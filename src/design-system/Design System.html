design system / 01 distilled from UUID Generator.html

Hairlines, mono, and one accent at a time.
A terminal-luxury system for developer tools.

Three families of token — color in OKLCH, type in Geist + Geist Mono, space on a 4px scale — plus a small library of components that earn every pixel: the workbench, the rail row, the UUID row, the kbd, the status bar.

§ 01 · principles

Six rules that earn the rest.

non-negotiables
01
Earn every pixel.
Real estate is leased to content, not ornament. Borders are 1px hairlines, never decorative frames.
02
One accent at a time.
A single hue carries focus, active state, glow, and CTA. Switch by hue — never stack accents.
03
Mono is the body.
UUIDs, keys, status, kbd — Geist Mono with a slashed zero. Sans (Geist) is reserved for display and prose.
04
Lowercase keys, UPPERCASE flags.
Rail keys (version, batch) are tracked lowercase. State flags (UPPER, STRIP) are uppercase.
05
Motion as feedback.
Hover swaps in 120ms, modals settle in 220ms, regenerate spins for ~380ms. No springs, no parallax.
06
Keyboard is the primary input.
Every action has a shortcut. The status bar advertises ? for the full list. Hover is a hint, not a requirement.

§ 06 · motion

Short. Purposeful. No bounces.

cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.7, 0.3, 1)
--d-swap
120ms
Hover and active state swaps. The default UI feedback duration.
--d-modal
220ms
Modal slide-and-scale entry on the standard easing.
--d-regen
380ms
Regenerate spin window. Just long enough to register a refresh.
